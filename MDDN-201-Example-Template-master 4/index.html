Networks seen in Aro Valley

By Gabriela Balulovska

Cables

Cables on power lines are seen all throughout Wellington and Aro Valley, these are allocated on power lines in order to deliver power to buildings and homes. The use of these help to distribute electricity across large distances, allowing us to connect to the internet.

Cameras

Cameras are typically seen around public areas all across Wellington City, mostly places on roofs of stores and big buildings. Cameras are used to capture images or video evidence of people and surroundings. With the use of power and electricity, cameras are constantly operating and recording footage.

Manholes

Electricity Manholes are seen basically on every street around the city to allow connections across the country and world. Manholes are the access point for many things, including the electricity we need for cables that allow connection for our systems.

Antenna

Antennas are commonly seen on houses in order to send electrical signals and radiofrequencys. They enable networks for the use of television and wireless internet access.
